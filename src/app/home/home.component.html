About

I am a PhD student at the Centre for Doctoral Training in Mathematics of Random Systems at the University of Oxford. I am part of the Probability Group in the Department of Statistics. My advisor is Julien Berestycki. Before coming to Oxford, I completed my B.A. and M.A. in mathematics at McGill University. During my master's degree, I worked under the supervision of Louigi Addario-Berry and Jessica Lin. For a full curriculum vitae, see cv.
